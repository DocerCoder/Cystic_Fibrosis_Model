💡 Upgrade Path: Add optional fields for omics data or simulate their impact on severity scores.
2. Longitudinal Patient Modeling
Liner cites models that track CF progression over time using multi-visit data.
Stratacyst currently uses snapshot phenotype inputs—no time-series or progression simulation.
💡 Upgrade Path: Introduce a timeline slider or multi-visit simulation to show disease trajectory.
3. CFTR Modulator Response Prediction
Liner references models that predict how patients will respond to modulators like Trikafta based on phenotype and genotype.
Stratacyst estimates eligibility, but doesn’t simulate treatment response or efficacy.
💡 Upgrade Path: Add a “modulator response simulator” based on published efficacy data by mutation class.
