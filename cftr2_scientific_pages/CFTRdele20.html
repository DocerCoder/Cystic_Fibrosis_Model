Summary Information

The variant CFTRdele20 is seen in 6 patients in our worldwide database. This variant is expected to result in CF.

The diagnosis of any individual patient with CF should be made based upon clinical parameters. The content of this website should not be used as a substitute for clinical judgement.

Functional Testing for CFTRdele20

This is a large insertion or deletion variant. Variants of this type may affect CFTR quantity.

Population Analysis

Penetrance analysis was not performed for specific variant combinations. The Penetrance analysis below is shown for each variant individually.

Penetrance analysis for CFTRdele20

Analysis of the general population and of CF carriers indicates that CFTRdele20 has no evidence of non-penetrance. This means that according to population frequency data, CFTRdele20 likely causes CF when combined with another CF-causing variant.
