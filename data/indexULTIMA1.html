Welcome to Stratacyst

Now with Bayesian posterior risk, SK-mapped clinical severity, and quantile-anchored severity percentile — all catalog-backed and audit-logged.

What it does

Fuses phenotype, genotype, microbiome, and catalogs into risk, severity, eligibility, and simulated response.

Why it helps

Interpretable outputs tied to sources: CFTR2, lung cohorts, infection bands, nutrition, and survival trends.

How to use

Enter phenotype ± genotype in “New patient,” compute, review rationale, export report, optionally send to EHR via FHIR.
